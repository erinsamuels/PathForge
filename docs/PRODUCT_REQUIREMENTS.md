# PathForge MVP (Version 1)

## Target User

PathForge is designed for students, recent graduates, and early-career professionals who have a career goal but are unsure how to get there.

## Problem

Career advice today is fragmented. Resumes tell employers about you, LinkedIn shows individual careers, and job boards show openings, but none answer the question:

**"What path did people like me take to reach the career I want?"**

## User Workflow

1. The user uploads a resume or CV.
2. Optionally, they connect a LinkedIn profile or provide a personal website.
3. The user specifies a target company, role, or career goal.
4. PathForge constructs a unified Career Profile from the uploaded information.
5. PathForge compares the user's Career DNA against professionals who have successfully reached similar goals.
6. The platform identifies the strongest career matches, analyzes the similarities and differences, and recommends the highest-impact next steps.

## User Receives

* Career timeline comparisons with similar professionals
* Career DNA similarity scores broken down by category
* Opportunity gap analysis (skills, experiences, certifications, and projects)
* Recommended next steps based on real career trajectories
* Suggested professionals to learn from and connect with

## Version 1 Will NOT Include

* Automatic messaging or outreach
* AI-generated networking conversations
* Interview tracking
* Salary tracking
* Market trend analysis
* Notifications
* Full job application management

Version 1 focuses on one core promise:

**Help users understand the path from where they are today to where they want to be by learning from people who have already made that journey.**
