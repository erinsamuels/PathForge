# PathForge Design System

## Vision

PathForge helps ambitious engineers discover the fastest, most proven path to their dream company.

Our goal is not to overwhelm users with data.

Our goal is to provide confidence in the next career decision.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Guide, Don't Overwhelm

Every screen should answer one simple question:

"What should I do next?"

Avoid clutter.

Avoid unnecessary metrics.

Prioritize clarity.

---

## 2. Explain Every Recommendation

Users should understand *why* PathForge recommends a company, skill, or career move.

Recommendations must always be backed by evidence.

---

## 3. Progress Over Perfection

PathForge celebrates progress.

The interface should make users feel like they are moving forward—not being judged.

Path Score is about direction, not grades.

# Product Philosophy

Users should always know:

• Where they are today

• Where they want to go

• The highest-probability path between those two points

Everything else supports those three ideas.
